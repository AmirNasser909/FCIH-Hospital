Anesthesia and Pain Management

  1. Anesthesia is the process by which the sensation or feeling of pain is reduced or removed permanently

  2. The Department of Anesthesiology of the International Peace Hospital provides all types of anesthesia, both local anesthesia and the total, as required by the anesthesia site and its way. This varies from just anesthesia wound area before the work of stitches to the anesthesia, which precedes the most complex surgery. Anesthesia is administered by injection, inhalation or both, allowing the doctor to perform diagnostic or surgical procedures safely and painlessly. Local anesthesia is used to facilitate the operation of laparoscopic surgeons and other physicians without unnecessarily subjecting the patient to general anesthesia. All our anesthesiologists have the competence, experience and commitment to provide the best service to the patient

  3. The anesthesiologist examines the patient to determine ways to help control the pain - each according to his or her individual condition - which is effective for patients with chronic pain or cancer.
